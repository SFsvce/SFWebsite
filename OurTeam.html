Assistant Professor, HSS

Speakers' Forum of SVCE provides a platform to the students to use English in a casual setting. A group of people or club members meet regularly to practice speaking and listening. Speakers' Forum also motivates the students to take part in various Inter-college debates and Model United Nations (MUN). A Mentorship program has been initiated by the members of the club in order to help those who find English challenging and are not able to communicate even their basic interests.
